About Masjid Al-Hidayah

Serving the community since 1995

Our History

Established in 1995 as The Tri-City Islamic Centre, Masjid Al-Hidayah (ISBC) originally operated from a 500 sq. ft. rented office. Through the dedication of our community and the blessings of Allah, we have grown to serve Muslim families across seven cities in the Greater Vancouver Area.

Our permanent mosque building officially opened on August 15, 2003, designed by award-winning Muslim architect Studio Senbel Architecture + Design. The building draws inspiration from traditional Islamic architecture while being adapted for the British Columbia climate.

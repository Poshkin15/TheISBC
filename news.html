Al-Hidayah Islamic School 2025-2026 Programs

Registrations are Now Open!

Give Your Child the Gift of Faith, Knowledge, and Strength on Weekends!

Weekend Islamic School (Grades K-7)

Saturdays and Sundays from 10 AM to 2 PM

Imagine your child spending their weekends not just learning, but truly growing – in faith, knowledge, and character. Our Weekend Islamic School is designed for children aged 5-12, offering a unique blend of spiritual learning and active engagement.

Program includes:

  • Dedicated time to Read and Memorize the Qur'an, fostering a deep spiritual bond
  • Expert instruction in Qur'an Tajweed, perfecting their recitation
  • Comprehensive Islamic Studies to build a strong moral compass
  • Foundational Arabic Language skills for a deeper understanding of Islam
  • Engaging Physical Education and other exciting activities that build discipline, confidence, and physical well-being

El Kutab School (Ages 13-17)

Girls: Sundays 10 AM - 2 PM
Boys: Sundays 10 AM - 2 PM

Designed for youth to grow in faith, knowledge, and character with Qur'an reading, Tajweed, and Arabic Language.

Parking problems are expected throughout the month due to the increased attendance during this blessed month. Please be courteous to others and observe the following guidelines:

  • Follow traffic volunteer instructions
